Onboarding FAQs:
Q: What is onboarding?
A: Onboarding is the chance for potential clients to meet with thier prospective life coach.
Q: What is the formatting of onboarding
A: Onboarding is a 30 minute zoom call where a client talk to a coach face-to-face.
Q: What will happen during an onboarding?
A: An onboarding call will consist of a short overview of major topics you would like to discuss regaring your life, a summary of expectations on both sides, and evaluation of general compatability as a client and service provide.
Q: What if onboarding goes bad?
A: Not all coaches and clients will be compatible! I will happily help you find someone who fits your needs better!
Q: What is the onboarding fee?
A: An onboarding meeting is free!
